Orlando Hidden Gems

by Melanie Priest

Looking for some new spots to try in the Orlando area? Well, you've come to the right place! Continue reading to see three of my favorite hidden gems in Orlando that the tourist crowds somehow haven't discovered yet.

Sampaguita Ice Cream

This Filipino inspired ice cream shop offers a wide variety of deliciously unique flavors! It has numerous vegan options and they make their ice cream in-house. You can also find fun things like Halo Halo, Bananacue, and Ube Brownie Sundaes on their menu. I highly recommend trying their Ube sauce!

Food: 10/10
Interior Design: 10/10
Service: 8/10

Koi Sushi

This Japanese restaurant has the best sushi I've had in Orlando hands down, and some of the best sushi I've had in general! You can tell that their ingredients are incredibly fresh and high quality. If you're a sushi lover, this place is a must. My favorite roll of theirs is the Lover Tuna Roll!

Food: 10/10
Interior Design: 7/10
Service: 10/10

Check out Koi Sushi's website here!

Infusion Tea

This tea spot is perfect for brunch with friends! Their tea menu is impressive and the atmosphere is quaint and cozy. Their Earl Grey Cream tea always tastes delicious and they make the best avocado toast I've ever had. Additionally, they offer a service where you can share a pot of tea and 3-tier pastry tray with a friend!

Food: 8/10
Interior Design: 8/10
Service: 9/10
